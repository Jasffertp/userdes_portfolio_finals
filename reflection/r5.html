R5: VISUAL INFORMATION DESIGN

Last week we were given an assignment to do, it was basically an invitation that miss wanted us to edit to make it more ‘appropriate’ for the theme of the letter. My first impression when I first saw the invitation was ‘wow, this is ugly’, those are the words that passed my thought as I was looking at the text, I didn’t want to look at it for long and it reminded me of an essay that I would just submit for a reflection paper.

I tried to make it look appropriate, I change the line breaks and put emphasis for the event. It was very fun and my thought process for designing it was what kind of design should I put that would make me want to go to the event? It was interesting because I wanted to make the color of the invitation black and the add gold borders to the invitation to make it look ‘fancy’ since as I was reading the invitation it was a call for a celebration party, I thought that maybe the sender had something that was worth celebrating for, and that’s how I basically thought of designing the invitation.

We discussed our individual design in class, and I found out that design was not only the color or the things that we put in the invitation card. Appropriate spacing and font face was also key in ensuring that the design of the card would not feel too heavy. Miss discussed to us what are the properties of design in fonts and in websites. What I really liked is the white space, when I was looking at websites that have less design in them and only the necessary things that should be in the website made me feel calm, I did not need to focus on other things and the design made me feel that and it was very cool for me, I liked design that are minimal and utilizes white space to make the website feel whole.

In the end we learned about the techniques that can be used when designing, like starting with a gray colored object and filling it with color as we go or the proper text alignment and height that would not make the website too tight nor too loose. The lesson was very insightful and it really helped me improved and consider what are the things that should go in my website and what should not.
