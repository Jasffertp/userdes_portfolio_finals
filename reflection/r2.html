R2: INTRO TO DESIGN THINKING PROCESS

I did not really think about design that much, and during the times that I would think about it, I would often think about design that was only very pleasing to me. However, with the discussion I realized that design was not only something that is visually pleasing, design is also concerned with the object or machines’ function. Now I cannot tell if a product has good designs just because of the looks. The lesson really helped me understand the what it means to design something, back whenever we were given a problem we were not really concerned with how we will be designing the solution rather we were only concerned with how we’ll be able to solve it, once we’ve had a solution we would just dive right into programming and not think about how it might look like, it was different with UI/UX of course but to not be able to empathize with the users of our supposed solution would be terrible as programmers. The lesson really made me think different when approaching problems, it made me think about how a user would react when they used that solution, and would they be happy about it? The lesson really changed my view regarding my steps in solving problems and creating solutions and I think it helped me become a better programmer.
