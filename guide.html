User Design

Design is how the object would function, it can also be the overall look or aesthetic of an object. Design can also mean trying to create or transform a product to the most desired object to the eye of a customer or to the market. Design is a plan to make something new for the people, that they perceive as beneficial (Looijesteijn, 2019). When somebody says “I’ll be designing the ….” They don’t mean they’ll be responsible for how the product looks but rather how the product will function, design is to make something, and that something can be used to solve a problem that hasn’t been solved or to solve a problem in a new and innovative way....

What is design thinking

Design thinking is an iterative process which is used to understand the users’ problems, address their problems and challenge and redefine the current problems through creating new and innovative solutions through prototyping and testing (Interaction Design Foundation, n.d.). It involves five phases: Empathy, Define, Ideate, Prototype, and Test, even though these are five phases of design thinking it’s not always sequential, teams would often go back to the different phases and iterate their solutions in an iterative manner. What is prototyping

Prototyping is the stage in the development of your idea in which you have a conversation with your idea, by directly understanding what the problems are and identifying what needs to be done. It is an experimental process where teams implement ideas into tangible forms, from paper to digital (Interaction Design Foundation). Prototyping is also a way of representing what your idea is.
